About One Chance Foundation

One Chance Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to saving the lives of children and vulnerable people across Africa who are suffering from severe illnesses, life-threatening conditions, extreme poverty, and urgent medical needs. We provide emergency surgeries, treatments, hospital care, nutrition, and hope — one life at a time.
